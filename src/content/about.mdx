# About Boycott Israel

## Our Mission

Our mission is to empower consumers to make informed decisions about the products they purchase. We believe that economic pressure through boycotting can be an effective tool for promoting human rights and justice.

## Our Team

We are a diverse group of activists, developers, and researchers committed to providing accurate information about products with ties to Israel and ethical alternatives.

## FAQ

### What is the BDS movement?

The Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions (BDS) movement works to end international support for Israel's oppression of Palestinians and pressure Israel to comply with international law.

### How do you verify product information?

We use a combination of public records, company statements, and verified sources to ensure our information is accurate. Our verification process includes multiple checks before any product is added to our database.
